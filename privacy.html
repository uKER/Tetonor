Privacy Policy

Tetonor — Last updated: 2 March 2026

Tetonor is a number puzzle game. This policy explains what information the app does and does not collect.

Information We Collect

None. Tetonor does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information. The app has no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no network connectivity. All game activity takes place entirely on your device.

Third-Party Services

Tetonor does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

Because Tetonor collects no data from anyone, it is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children or adults.

Changes to This Policy

If the app ever changes in a way that affects privacy, this page will be updated and the date above will reflect the change.
